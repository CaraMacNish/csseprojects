Project Topic Suggestions
Below are suggested project topics for CSSE Honours (BSc Hons, BPhil, BACS) and Master of Data Science (Coursework and Dissertation) students, as well as other students where applicable. Some topics may be suitable to be undertaken by more than one student.

Note that Software Engineering and other Engineering projects are distributed through the School of Engineering, so the listings here are for information only.

More recently added or updated project topics are listed first. Some topics may have been assigned previously, however similar or additional projects may be offered. Please confirm availability with supervisors.

The topics listed also indicate current research areas of staff interest. Staff may be willing to supervise student-proposed projects in allied areas to those shown if they regard the project to have a suitable research component.
